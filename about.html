Who We Are

A Beloved Mumbai
Institution

Gourmet Garden is a well-established vegetarian dining destination in Bandra West, Mumbai. We have been the go-to spot for every craving — breakfast, lunch, and late-night dinner.

We serve 4 complete cuisines — North Indian, South Indian, Chinese & Mumbai Fast Food — all under one roof, at prices that feel fair every single time.
